peAR Technologies is an AR / VR tech based Start-up in Mumbai. Their first product peAR-1 shows how the food on menu will look like on the table of your restaurant. I Worked here as a photographer. My job was to provide them with images of food dishes from various angles so that they can render those images and build a 3D model (Virtual/Augmented Reality). Brings your food to life.
